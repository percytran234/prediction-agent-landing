// FAQ

Honest answers to hard questions.

Does this guarantee profit?

No. The signal engine is an untested hypothesis. We promise disciplined risk management, not returns. Even at 50% win rate, your account is protected from blowups.

How much to start?

$50 minimum on free tier. Agent bets 2% per round ($1 on $50). Scale up as you get comfortable.

Is my money safe?

Funds stay on Polygon, not in our database. Withdraw to your wallet anytime. We never custody your keys.

How is this different from gambling?

Agent SKIPS when signals are weak. Fixed 2% bet size. Hard stop-losses by code. Gamblers do none of these.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No lock-in, no contracts. Stop and withdraw whenever. Free tier never expires.

What's the performance fee?

Percentage of realized profit only. Zero profit = zero fee. We only earn when you earn.
