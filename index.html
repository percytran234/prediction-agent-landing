// Three Layers of Protection

The edge isn't prediction.
It's discipline.

Whether the signal engine hits 51% or 55%, these three layers protect your capital.

2%

Fixed Bet Size

Every round: exactly 2% of bankroll. One loss = $2 on $100. Even 10 consecutive losses only costs 20%. No account blowup.

60%

Skip Threshold

Agent only bets when confidence ‚â• 60%. Below that? SKIP. Skipping 40-50% of rounds is correct. Not betting = not losing.

-10%

Hard Stop-Loss

Daily loss limit: -10%. Consecutive losses: 4 ‚Üí pause 1h. Profit target: +5% ‚Üí lock gains. All enforced by code, not willpower.
